⚠ This game is still in alpha. Content and balance may change at any time.
The changelog is not actively maintained at the moment.

---

v0.8
• Race system: once the first row of divine powers is complete, each Ascension lets you choose the race of your next cycle, each with its own strengths, weaknesses and unique racial building.
• Infinitely repeatable researches: some late-game researches that boost prestige points earned can now be relaunched indefinitely, with a cost that doubles each time
• New Ascension statistics screen: history of recent ascensions, best game records (prestige, ascension), max island tier and corruption reached, total playtime and races played
• Many new endgame researches, along with the matching vertices on the prestige map

---

v0.7
• New endgame Tier system: generated NPC civilizations grow progressively stronger based on your total accumulated prestige points, with a matching prestige gain bonus
• Destroying an enemy civilization now grants a prestige gain bonus
• New researches, including several that boost prestige points earned
• Market simplification: removed market specialization and manual resource buying
• Maritime Beacons: Great Lighthouse level 2 unlocks building beacons out at sea, letting you extend maritime routes beyond the coast

---

v0.6
• Reworked enemy behavior
• Enriched tooltips: resource production and consumption are now displayed
• Improved statistics screen: completed researches (cross-prestige total) on the first row, cities / buildings / total levels / unique buildings on the second row, and an optional third row

v0.5

• Abyss Gate: ultimate monument, an evolution of the Corruption Spire

• Time speed control
