v0.2
The Awakening of Magic: the southern branch of the prestige map is dedicated to developing magic around the Ley Lines hex. Fairy Circles and Dolmens appear on the island and generate crystals, the first source of this resource even before the Underworld. The Secret of Magic unlocks the Mage Tower and a new Rituals screen: launch and maintain rituals by consuming crystals — their effect grows linearly with power, but their cost rises with its square. The number of towers caps active rituals, the sum of their levels caps total power. Six rituals to discover through research, from Growth to the Light of the Depths, boosting production, armies and Underworld exploration.
The Age of Steel: the north-east branch of the prestige map grows beyond the Steel Secret with five new vertices and the Steelworks hex. Two new buildings appear: the Blast Furnace (a unique building boosting all Smelters) and the Arsenal. New Steel Armor mechanic: soldiers lost in combat can be saved by consuming Steel. Five new advanced technologies complete the branch, including Rail Logistics (faster reinforcements) and Special Steels (selling Steel at the market).
The Descent into the Underworld: the north-west branch of the prestige map opens the way to the depths. The Deepest Mine becomes a grand dig site placed on a Mountain like a Wonder: invest your resources (including 1000 Steel) to break through to the Underworld. Below ground, Mushroom Caves feed your outposts through the new Mushroom Farm, treasures await explorers, and Mithril can finally be extracted with the Mithril Mine. But the Underworld is violent: regenerating trolls and devastating ogres roam the tunnels. Four new technologies accompany the descent, from Speleology to Mithril Tools.

v0.1
Three islands are available, each procedurally generated. A tutorial guides new players through the core mechanics. Military combat lets you defend your cities against bandits and dragons. The first prestige level is unlockable, granting permanent bonuses that carry over between runs. A research tree provides progressive upgrades to your civilization.
